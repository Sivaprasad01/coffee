Handcrafted Curations

  • Affogato

    Okay, maybe I was wrong. Coffee and ice cream is better. Is it? You decide! Ideal serving: 1 scoop vanilla ice cream + 30ml Espresso poured on the top in a 150 ml cup.
  • Frappé

    One of the fanciest coffee drinks out there, and the one that contains the least amount of coffee in comparison to other items. This Starbucks favourite has to be served cold to be enjoyed.
  • Vienna Mocha

    Or simply known as Vienna Coffee, this coffee is a fun-loving twist to your regular Espresso shot. What’s the twist, you ask? WHIPPED CREAM! Ideal serving: 60ml Espresso + Whipped cream on the top in a 150ml cup.
  • Irish

    Ideal serving: 5g (or 1 tsp.) brown sugar + 120ml French press coffee + 60ml Irish whiskey + 75ml heavy cream (in that order) in a 250ml glass.
  • Cappuccino

    Everyone s favourite and the most well-known and standard coffee drink, cappuccino contains more milk-to-coffee ratio. Ideal serving: 60ml Espresso + 60ml steamed milk + 60ml foamed milk (in that order) in a 200ml cup.
